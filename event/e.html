Event Details

Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Location: vrindavan theme park, Holi Festival

About The Event

Join us for an unforgettable Holi celebration filled with colors, music, dance, and delicious food. Holi is the Indian festival of colors that marks the arrival of spring and the triumph of good over evil.

Our event features traditional Holi activities, live performances, DJ sets, and authentic Indian cuisine. Come dressed in white and prepare to be covered in vibrant colors as we celebrate together!

Note: All colors used are natural, non-toxic, and skin-friendly.
