About Me

Hi, I'm Bhavani Vuppuloju, a photographer based in Hyderabad.

For over a time, I've been capturing wild life's most precious moments through my lens. My journey in photography began during college when I first picked up a camera to document my forest traveling across Nallamala. What started as a hobby quickly evolved into a passion and eventually, a fulfilling career.

My work spans various genres including landscape, mountains, trees, and a photographynimals. I'm particularly drawn to the interplay of light and shadow, finding beauty in both grand vistas and intimate details that often go unnoticed.

When I'm not behind the camera, I enjoy teaching photography workshops and mentoring emerging photographers. I believe that photography is not just about technical skill but about developing a unique visual voice and perspective.
