Appetizers

Samosa

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large Potatoes
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green Chili
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 2 tbsp Chopped Cilantro
  • Wonton Wrappers or Samosa Pastry

Instructions:

  • Boil and mash the potatoes. Set aside.
  • Fry onions and green chilies in oil with cumin and spices.
  • Mix in the mashed potatoes and cilantro. Let it cool.
  • Fill samosa wrappers with the mixture, seal, and deep fry until golden brown.
  • Serve hot with tamarind or mint chutney.

Main Course

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1/4 cup Urad Dal (split black lentils)
  • 1/4 cup Toor Dal (pigeon peas)
  • 1 Potato (boiled)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Curry Leaves
  • Green Chilies

Instructions:

  • Soak rice and dals overnight, then grind into a smooth batter.
  • Ferment the batter overnight for the dosa consistency.
  • For the filling, fry onions, mustard seeds, turmeric, and curry leaves in oil, then add boiled potatoes and spices.
  • Pour a thin layer of batter onto a hot griddle, cook until crispy, and then add the potato filling. Fold and serve with coconut chutney and sambar.

Hyderabadi Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken or Mutton
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • Cilantro and Mint Leaves
  • Saffron (optional)

Instructions:

  • Marinate the chicken/mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  • Fry onions until golden brown. Layer the cooked meat, rice, and fried onions.
  • Cook the biryani using the dum (steaming) method, layering it in a sealed pot over low heat for about 30 minutes.
  • Serve with raita and salad.

Desserts

Payasam (Kheer)

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Rice (or Vermicelli)
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/4 cup Cashews and Raisins
  • Cardamom Powder

Instructions:

  • Roast the rice (or vermicelli) in ghee until golden.
  • Add milk and cook on low heat until the mixture thickens.
  • Stir in sugar, cardamom, and roasted nuts. Simmer until creamy.
  • Serve warm or chilled.

Coconut Ladoo

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Desiccated Coconut
  • 1/2 cup Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • Ghee for greasing

Instructions:

  • Heat milk in a pan, then add sweetened condensed milk and cardamom.
  • Stir in desiccated coconut and cook until it thickens.
  • Let the mixture cool, then shape into small balls (ladoos) and let them set.
  • Serve chilled or at room temperature.
